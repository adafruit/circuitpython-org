The senseBox MCU Eye is an ESP32-S3 board designed for camera and TinyML
projects. Its 16 MB of flash and 8 MB of PSRAM provide room for image processing
and small AI models directly on the board.

The board includes a camera, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth LE, LoRa, a 6-axis
accelerometer, a microSD card slot, an RGB LED, and a battery charger. A QWIIC
compatible I2C connector and additional GPIO ports make it easy to add sensors
and other hardware.

Hardware files, examples, and additional documentation are available in the
[senseBox Eye GitHub repository](https://github.com/sensebox/senseBox_Eye).
