The Waveshare ESP32-S3-Tiny-N8R8 is a compact ESP32-S3 development board with
8 MB of flash and 8 MB of PSRAM. It exposes 34 GPIOs while keeping the main
board small by placing USB-C and the BOOT and RESET buttons on a detachable
adapter board connected by an 8-pin FPC cable.

The board also includes a WS2812 RGB LED and supports 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi and
Bluetooth LE. It is available with or without the USB adapter board.
